Student Performance in Mid Term Exam

This dashboard showcases the scores attained by students in each question of MYE respectively. This is to show the breakdown of scores for each component. This assumes that each question tests one or multiple concepts, if one question has more people who score lower marks, this means that students are weak at that particular concept. This helps us to understand which concept students are weak on.
